Hot-Plug and Controlled Hot Removal Capability

One operational feature of the Zen4 system is the hot-plug and controlled hot removal capability.

All of the modules used in the Zen4 system have circuitry to allow removal and insertion of a module without interrupting system power. This is accomplished by controlling the inrush current and power sequencing on the boards.
